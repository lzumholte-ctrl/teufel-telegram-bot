[DIS Magazine](https://dismagazine.com/) DIS is a multimedia art magazine. DIS
is a dissection of fashion and commerce which seeks to dissolve conventions,
distort realities, disturb ideologies, dismember the establishment, and disrupt
the dismal dissemination of fashion discourse that's been distinctly distributed
in order to display the disenfranchised as disposable. All is open to
discussion. There is no final word. DIS is Lauren Boyle, Solomon Chase, Marco
Roso, Nick Scholl, and David Toro.
